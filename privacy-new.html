NewPet - Privacy
New Pet is committed to protecting the personal information and privacy of users of the New Pet service
(collectively, Users or You). We may collect and use personal information about you when you use the
New Pet Service. We would like to explain to you through the New Pet Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to
as the Policy) the rules governing the handling of personal information collected and used by us, as well as
the ways we provide you with access to, correction of, deletion of, and protection of such personal information,
in order to better protect your rights and interests.
I. How we collect and use your information We will follow the principles of justification, lawfulness and necessity
to collect and use the information you voluntarily provide in the course of using our products (or services) based
on the following functions and services.
Registration of accounts When you register and log in to your New Pet account, you provide your mobile
phone number to register as required, and this information is only used for the purpose of user login to the
software.
How we share, transfer and disclose personal information
Sharing We take the protection of your personal information very seriously. We will not share your personal
information with any third party outside of us and our affiliates without your consent, except in the following
circumstances:
Providing our services to you. We may share your information with our partners and other third parties to
achieve the function of the New Pet service and allow you to use the services you need normally. For
example: payment institutions that provide payment services, partners that provide data services (including
online advertising monitoring, data statistics, data analysis), third-party logistics companies;
To maintain and improve our services. We may share your personal information with partners and other
third parties to help us provide you with a more targeted and improved service, for example: communication
service providers who send emails or push notifications on our behalf;
Sharing with third parties, such as partners who have commissioned us to carry out promotions, for the
purpose of enabling such commissioners to understand the reach and effectiveness of the promotions. For
example, we may inform the commissioning party how many people have viewed their promotional
information or purchased the commissioning partys goods after seeing such information, or provide them
with statistical information that is not personally identifiable to help them understand their audience or
customers;
Necessary Sharing with Affiliated Parties. In order for us to provide you with consistent services based on a
unified account system, as well as to facilitate your unified management, personalised recommendations,
and to protect system and account security, your personal information may be shared between us and our
affiliates as necessary.
To fulfil the other purposes described in the section How we collect and use personal information in
Article 1 of this Policy;
To fulfil our obligations and exercise our rights under this policy or any other agreement we have with you;
To the extent permitted by law or regulation, we may exchange information with other companies and
organisations in order to comply with the law, to safeguard us and our affiliates or partners, you or other
New Pet users, or the general public from harm to their interests, property or safety, for example, in order to
prevent illegal activities such as fraud and to reduce credit risk. However, this does not include information
that is sold, rented, shared or otherwise disclosed for profit in violation of the commitments made in this
policy;

Protection of personal information We take information security very seriously and have established a
dedicated security team and take all reasonably practicable steps to protect your personal information:
Technical measures for data security We will adopt security measures in line with industry standards,
including the establishment of reasonable institutional norms, security technology to prevent unauthorised
access, use, modification of your personal information, to avoid data damage or loss. Our Services adopt a
variety of encryption technologies, for example, in certain New Pet Services, we will encrypt and store your
personal information using encryption technologies (such as SSL) and segregate it using segregation
technologies in order to protect your personal information. When personal information is used, such as
information display and information association calculation, we use a variety of data desensitisation
techniques to enhance the security of the information in use. We also use strict data access control and
multiple authentication technology to protect personal information from unauthorised use of data.
Other security measures we take to protect personal information We have an industry-advanced data
security management system that focuses on data and revolves around the data lifecycle, which enhances
the security of the entire system in multiple dimensions from organisational construction, system design,
personnel management, and product technology. We manage and regulate the storage and use of personal
information through the establishment of a data classification and grading system, data security
management norms, and data security development norms. We provide full security control of data through
information contact confidentiality agreements, monitoring and auditing mechanisms. We also organise
security and privacy training courses to enhance employees security awareness and knowledge of personal
information protection policies and procedures.

incident by email, letter, phone call, push notification, etc. When it is difficult to inform the subject of the
